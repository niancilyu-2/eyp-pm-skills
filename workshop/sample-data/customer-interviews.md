# Tideflow — Customer Interview Notes

Tideflow is a team collaboration & docs app. Q2 2026. Four accounts. Quotes verbatim.

## Interview 1 — Helix Studios (Creative agency, Enterprise)
Bills clients by the hour across ~15 retainers. Tracks time in a spreadsheet alongside Tideflow,
then reconciles by hand.
- "Tideflow is where the work happens, but I can't log a single hour against it. So every invoice
  starts in Excel."
- "Cadence does time tracking natively — that's why our ops team keeps pushing to switch."
Asks: native time tracking against tasks/projects; export to billing.

## Interview 2 — Northpeak Consulting (Consulting, Enterprise)
30 consultants. Main pain is not knowing who is over- or under-booked next sprint.
- "I can't answer 'who has capacity in two weeks' without DMing ten people."
- "We're piloting Cadence purely for resource/capacity planning."
Asks: capacity/resource planning view; utilization by person.

## Interview 3 — Saffron Labs (Biotech, Mid-Market)
Happy with collaboration. Pain is finding old documents.
- "Search is the thing. We have three years of protocols and I can never find the right one."
Asks: better search/filtering.

## Interview 4 — Orchard Health (Healthcare, Standard, small)
Small team, mostly happy. Field staff want a better phone experience.
- "Honestly we just need the mobile app to not be painful."
Asks: improved mobile. (Counter-signal: small account, different need.)
