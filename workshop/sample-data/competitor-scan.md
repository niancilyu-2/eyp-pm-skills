# Tideflow — Competitor Scan (Q2 2026)

Two competitors come up repeatedly in churn/at-risk conversations: **Cadence** and **Orbit** (both
team-work platforms with built-in operations features). Tideflow wins on docs/collaboration UX.

| Capability | Tideflow | Cadence | Orbit |
|------------|----------|---------|-------|
| Docs & collaboration UX | Strong | Moderate | Moderate |
| Native time tracking | None | Full | Full |
| Capacity / resource planning | None | Full | Partial |
| Utilization reporting | None | Full | Partial |
| Search | Weak | Moderate | Moderate |
| Mobile app | Basic | Full | Full |
| Price | Lower | Higher | Mid |

**Cadence** — positions on "run your services business": time tracking, capacity planning, and
utilization are core. Trade-off: heavier, pricier, weaker writing/docs experience.

**Orbit** — time tracking is solid; capacity planning is partial. Mid-priced.
