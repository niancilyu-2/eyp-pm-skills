# Product goals (for pm-ideation)

Optional context input for `pm-ideation` — pass it alongside the data folder
(`/pm-ideation workshop/mock-data workshop/product-goals.md`). Like `team-capacity.md` for
`pm-plan-and-estimate`, this is workshop-level context, **not** an evidence leg: it says what the
product org is trying to move, so success criteria land in the org's own KPIs.

## What we optimize for (leading indicators first)
- **Customer satisfaction** with the editing/authoring experience (CSAT on key flows; NPS).
- **Adoption of new capabilities by non-developer roles** — % of customer teams where editors and
  marketers use a capability without developer help.
- **Time-to-value** — days from a capability shipping to first productive use per account.
- **Stickiness** — weekly active editors per account; breadth of feature use.

## Trailing outcomes these feed (influenced, not directly targeted)
- Net revenue retention; churn among at-risk segments; expansion revenue.

## How ideation should use this
Express success criteria in the leading KPIs above (with baseline · target · timeline), use
commercial figures ($ at risk, churn) as evidence, and say which trailing outcome each criterion
is expected to move.
